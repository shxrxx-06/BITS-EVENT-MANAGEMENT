About GatherUp

GatherUp is a platform designed to simplify and enhance group activity organization. Whether you're a student, faculty member, or administrator, GatherUp provides a seamless way to create, manage, and participate in events.

Key Features

  • Effortless event creation and management
  • User roles: Student, Faculty, and Admin
  • Secure login and authentication system
  • Personalized profile pages
  • Privacy-focused and user-friendly design

GatherUp aims to foster collaboration, engagement, and efficiency in organizing group events, making it easier for communities to come together.
